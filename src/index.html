FAQ

  • How to book a car on the website?

    Select the rental dates you are interested in in the calendar. The site will display cars available for rent for this period. By selecting one of the cars, you will be taken to its page. Next, by clicking on the Book button, all you have to do is provide your contact information and confirm the reservation.

  • What is the minimum age and driving experience required to rent a car?

    You can rent a car if you are over 21 years old.
    The minimum driving experience to rent a car must be 2 years.

  • What documents are required to rent a car?

    To rent a car you need to have a passport and a driver's license of the established form. If a car is rented by foreign citizens, in addition to a national driver's license, it is also necessary to have an international license.

  • How much mileage per day is included in the rental price?

    A rental day includes 200 km of mileage. At the same time, when booking a car for several days, the total mileage for the entire trip is taken into account, no matter what mileage you have on a given day of rental. If the mileage included in the trip is exceeded, each subsequent km will be calculated at a separate rate, which you can see on the page of the car you are interested in. The site also provides the opportunity to select options for additional or unlimited mileage.

  • Is the car insured and what may be my liability in the event of an accident?

    All company cars have MTPL (driver's civil liability to third parties) and CASCO insurance (car insurance in case of damage, accident or theft). The latter provides for a deductible, which is the minimum liability of the client in the event of an insured event.

  • Where can I receive/return a car?

    You can receive the car at the company’s office or order a car delivery service to your address. Delivery a car to the address is 10 USD. The car can also be returned to the company’s address or to your address (paid service - 10 USD)
